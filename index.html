About Us

The Soni Family Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to creating lasting change by supporting initiatives in education, healthcare, and community development. Founded in 2023, we believe in the power of collective action to transform lives.

Tax-Exempt Status: Donations to the Soni Family Foundation are tax-deductible under IRS Section 501(c)(3).
